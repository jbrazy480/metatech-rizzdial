# Annual Re-engagement

> GREEN = edit per business. RED (DO NOT EDIT) blocks marked.

## Project instructions / Request
Re-engage old leads using a highly compelling **annual offer** — stronger, more exclusive, more time-sensitive than normal.

NOT standard nurture. NOT a generic check-in. Limited annual campaign that creates responses by presenting a rare deal, exclusive promotion, bonus, increased discount, or one-time opportunity the lead cannot normally get.

People You Speak With:
- Previously showed interest in **BUSINESS NAME**
- Did not book, buy, or went cold
- May still have original problem
- May only respond if offer is unusually strong
- Not expecting a long call

Objectives:
- Reintroduce yourself and **BUSINESS NAME**
- Present annual offer early, make it sound exclusive
- Emphasize this is NOT a normal everyday offer
- Create urgency without sounding fake/aggressive
- Find out if they want to take advantage
- Move to transfer or booking immediately
- Exit cleanly if not interested

Offer Positioning Rules:
- Must feel rare
- Must feel valuable
- Must feel time-sensitive
- Waiting = losing access
- Focus on result/savings/bonus/advantage

Tone: Direct, confident, exciting, natural, urgent but credible. Never desperate, pushy, robotic.

Call Rules: Short, lead with reason quickly, offer = centerpiece, don't ramble, transfer/book if interested, don't force.

**Important Context:** Main driver is the strength of the offer, not a long educational conversation. Call should feel like: *"I'm reaching out because there's a rare opportunity available right now, and I wanted to make sure you had the chance to claim it before it's gone."*

## Script

🟢 **GREETING**
~"Hi, is this {{first_name}}?"

🟢 **REINTRODUCTION**
~"Hey {{first_name}}, this is **AGENT NAME** with **BUSINESS NAME**."

🟢 **REASON FOR CALL**
~"I'm reaching out because we're running a special annual offer right now, and it's not something we normally make available during the year. Have you already heard about it?"

🟢 **OFFER PRESENTATION**
~"Since you had looked into **PRODUCT / SERVICE** with us before, I wanted to make sure you had a chance to take advantage of it before it goes away."
~"Right now, the offer is **OFFER DETAILS / DISCOUNT / BONUS / SPECIAL TERMS**."

🟢 **INTEREST CHECK**
~"Would you be open to seeing if this makes sense for you while the offer is still available?"

🟡 **IF INTERESTED**
~"Perfect — the next best step would be to get you connected with the right person on our team while this is still active."

🟡 **IF LIVE TRANSFER AVAILABLE**
~"I can get you over to them now so you can take advantage of the offer."

🟡 **IF BOOKING BETTER**
~"Or I can help you lock in a quick time so you don't miss it."

🟠 **IF THEY ASK WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT**
~"The big reason people are responding is because this is stronger than what we normally offer during the year, so it gives you a better chance to get **RESULT / SAVINGS / BONUS** right now."

🟠 **IF INTERESTED BUT HESITANT**
~"Totally fair — I just didn't want you to miss something that could save you money / get you a better result / give you access to something we don't normally include."
~"Would it be easier to get you connected now, or set a quick time before the offer ends?"

🔴 **IF NOT INTERESTED**
~"No problem at all — I just wanted to make sure you knew this annual offer was available before it goes away."

🔴 **IF ALREADY BOUGHT/BOOKED/HANDLED**
~"Got it — glad you got that taken care of. I just wanted to make sure you didn't miss the offer in case it still helped."

🔴 **IF DON'T REMEMBER**
~"No worries — you had looked into **PRODUCT / SERVICE** with **BUSINESS NAME** before, and I'm just reaching out because we've got a rare offer available right now."

🟡 **IF ASKING WHO/WHAT COMPANY**
~"We help people with **PAIN POINT** so they can get **DESIRED RESULT**."

🟡 **IF ASKING WHY NOW**
~"Because this is an annual offer and it's only available for a limited time, so we're reaching out to past leads before it's gone."

🟡 **IF MORE INFO BEFORE MOVING FORWARD**
~"Absolutely — the main benefit here is **BENEFIT**, and the reason people are jumping on it is because this deal / bonus / discount is not normally available."
~"If it makes sense, I can get you connected now or help you reserve a quick time."
